MongoDb

NoSQL database MongoDB is renowned for its scalability and versatility. MongoDB allows for changeable schemas since it stores data in documents that resemble JSON, unlike conventional relational databases. Because of this, it's a great option for applications that need to store a variety of intricate data structures. Because of its easy scalability and excellent performance due to its document-oriented design, MongoDB is a popular choice for contemporary applications that need to handle massive amounts of data and quick development cycles.
